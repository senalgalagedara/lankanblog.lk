Our Expirince on Kehelpathdoruwa Mountain Hiking (Feb, 17-19,2024)

First of all I want to say this article written by Janidu Malith with his personal experinces. This may help you to improve your experience and safty

Start and our first destination

We started our journey from Panadura railway station at 4 in the morning. We Reached Kandy around 9 am. Our destination was Kalugala village. To go there, we boarded the bus from Kandy to Kalugala. When we reached the village, it was around 12:30 in the afternoon. We changed our clothes and had food before climb the mountain. Kalugala is a beautiful village. The villagers are very friendly. Those people said directons to climb the mountain.

We strated hike after changed clothes and had lunch at village

Hiking and Unforgettable experience

We started hiking around 2-3 p.m.There is no water on the mountain. So water was filled from a canal in the village. Kehelpatdorowa mountain cannot be climbed directly. After going through a forest and climbing about three more mountains, we have to climb to Kehelpatdoruva. ​​There were old elephant huts on the road we went to. Although there were elephants on that side, the elephants came during the harvest season. That's why we didn't have any problem. After two and a half hours, when it was around five o'clock, we were able to climb to the top of Kanaviddagala. The mountain after Kanaviddagala is Kehelpatdoruwa. But because of the fog, we camped at the top of Kanaviddagala. A dog came with us from the village to show us the way.

Kanaviddagala mountain While Hikiing Morning view

The next morning, we woke up around 6 a.m. and had noodles for breakfast. We cleaned and cleared the campsite and started our journey back to the village. This is a short story of our adventure. I couldn't share some information because we faced the worst. Hiking in the Knuckles is hard and dangerous. But I (we) will complete our destination next time. (The time from March to May is not good for hiking in the Knuckles. We knew this and faced the worst.)

We after placing tent on the top of the mountain
We cooking outside tent.
Bonfire and mountain

Next day and end of the journey

The next morning was even more beautiful. Before sunrise, the entire sky was purple. We took the camp and started descending. Even if there were leeches there was no problem. We reached the village in about two hours. But there was still time for the bus to arrive, so we bathed in a natural canal in the village. We ate the remaining noodles and went to Kandy by bus. We came to Panadura by train from Kandy. One of the most beautiful journeys of my life ended that way. I was sad because I couldn't go to the top of Kehelpatdoruwa, but I feel great when I think about the experiences we got on the trip.
